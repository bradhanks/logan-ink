# Annotated Reading List — Cancer Prevention, Population Science & Epidemiologic Methods

A curated, verified list of foundational works for someone (like Logan) early in a
cancer-prevention / population-science research path. Each entry has been confirmed via
WebSearch; sources are cited in the per-entry files. The "what Logan took from it" notes
are written honestly for an undergraduate / early-career reader — i.e., what's realistic
to absorb now, not a claim of mastery.

## Methods & causal reasoning

2. [Rothman, Greenland & Lash — *Modern Epidemiology*](./modern-epidemiology.md)
3. [Hernán & Robins — *Causal Inference: What If*](./hernan-robins-causal-inference.md)
